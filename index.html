Research Project

My PhD research focuses on integrating Artificial Intelligence into the entire risk management process to enhance effectiveness and efficiency at all stages. The core objective is to develop a comprehensive framework that leverages AI capabilities throughout the complete risk management lifecycle - from risk identification and assessment to mitigation planning and continuous monitoring - while maintaining human oversight and ethical standards.

Research Interests

My research interests focus on the intersection of Artificial Intelligence, Risk Management, Security Governance, and Cybersecurity. I am particularly interested in developing innovative methodologies that leverage the capabilities of generative AI to improve security and risk management processes, while always maintaining human supervision and ethical standards.

Key Areas:

  • Generative AI for cybersecurity applications
  • Explainable AI (XAI) in complex security contexts
  • Frugal AI systems for resource-constrained environments
  • Attack graph analysis and automated mitigation strategies
  • Human-AI collaboration in risk management
  • AI governance and ethical considerations in security applications
